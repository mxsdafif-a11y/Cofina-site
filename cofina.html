Our Flagship

The Spanish
Latte story.

It started as an experiment. A barista's curiosity — what happens when you replace sugar with condensed milk, and let the espresso speak through cream? The result stopped everyone in their tracks. It's been our number one ever since.

We serve it hot, iced, or in a 1-litre bottle to share. Because some things are too good to keep to yourself.
